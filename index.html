1 · Metro coverage

True isochrones follow the real street network. Fast mode (default) uses one point per station with the walking time padded by that station's exit spread — full city in ~15 requests, under a minute. High accuracy computes from all 225 exits (~4 min) and matters mostly for big multi-exit hubs. Results are cached; re-loads only fetch what's missing. Free key at openrouteservice.org.
