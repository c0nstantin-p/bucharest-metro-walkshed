4 · School walkshed

5 · Viable home zone

Where the metro and school walksheds overlap, in deep violet, drawn above both. With only one of them active, the zone is just that one; the intersection only appears once both are on.

6 · Custom address walkshed

    For any address outside the baked data — computes a live 15-min isochrone via ORS if you paste a key, or a simple radius circle without one. The metro and school walksheds above never call the API. Free key at openrouteservice.org. The key stays in this page only.

    7 · Neighborhood texture

    2,340 hexagons scored on greenness, crowding, traffic edge, park reach, air and built cover — each layer button below is its own legend (ramp, scale, and which direction is better). Hover a cell for the full readout, click to lock it. With a viable home zone active (section 5), the mesh clips to just that area.

    The weave blends five of the metrics into one score — greener is better all-round. Weights only affect it once it's on.
